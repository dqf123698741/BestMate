Service

Food Menu

For the food menu, we have developed special food menu for each type of pets to ensure a healthy and balanced diet for them.

Room Configuration

The BestMate Hotel has three types of customized rooms for each type of pets which matches the price of service respectively. The functions and

facilities in the room will improve with the upgrade of service.

Special Facilities

As to the facilities, our hotel provides gym, spa room and swimming pool for pets to enjoy. The gym has a variety of sport programs and professional

staff guide pets to enjoy exercise. In the spa room, all the spa staff have pet massager certification to make your pets suitable. For the swimming

pool, the water would have the most suitable temperature for your pets and the pool would be disinfected twice a day.

Medical Care

We have a professional pet doctor observing the pets and recording their condition. In case of any emergency, our doctor will also give emergency

treatment to pet who are in unfortunate condition with the first aid supplies stored in the hotel and send the pet to pet hospital if needed.
