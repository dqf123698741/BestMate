prices

Dogs

service type price
Silver Service $30 per dog per day
Gold Service $50 per dog per day
Diamond Service $70 per dog per day

The elderly, injured, disabled or sick pet dogs may be charged extra fees, but we definitely would do our best to take care of them.

Please Note

1. Before you send your pet to our BestMate hotel, please book in advance the type and number of days of service required, and let our friendly staff know in advance if your pet has any special conditions or habits.

2. Please book pet boarding services at least 2 days in advance to avoid inadequate service conditions or staff shortages at BestMate hotel.

3. If your pet needs the extended foster care, please contact our staff in time or we may charge additional fees if the pet stays longer than scheduled.
