Pet Boarding Insurance

What is boarding insurance

Pet boarding insurance is mainly designed to guarantee the rights and interests if accidents happen during the boarding process. If your pet suffers

the accidental injury or disease during the boarding process of BestMate hotel, the insurance would cover your pet's basic medical liability, potential

surgical treatment liability and provide the best service protection. If anything unfortunate happens, we will contact you as soon as possible and

try our best to help your pet. In addition, we will give you the compensation as the insurance required.

Why needs boarding insurance

When there are business trips, holidays, tourism and other occasions, the pet owners would consider sending their beloved pets to boarding care.

However, there still could be risk for the pets during the boarding care. The pets could have a stress response in an unfamiliar environment and

there could be some skin diseases and infectious diseases even though we will strictly disinfect the hotel every day. An insurance would be quite

perfect for the pet owners who choose to have your pets under our boarding care because it could protect rights for both you and the hotel and

avoid disputes if any unfortunate accidents happen. If your pet has any psychological or physical problems, we will send your pet safely to the

professional pet hospital designated by the insurance for medical treatment and reimburse your pet's medical consumption as stipulated by the

insurance.

What boarding insurance do we provide

The BestMate hotel cooperates with specialized pet insurance company which has boarding insurance for all accepted types of pets such as Dogs,

Cats, Rabbits, Rats, Birds etc. Please contact our friendly staff for more information on price or any probelms.
